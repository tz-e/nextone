NextOne Management was established in 2019 with the primary purpose of organizing workshops in the field of medicine.

We operate internationally and organize seminars that combine both training and exchange of experience with the best hospitals, with which we closely collaborate. This is what makes our method truly unique.

OUR ACTIVITY

We organize workshops held at the best Italian healthcare institutions for groups, mostly composed of practitioners and heads of departments, registered in the hospitals of Moscow.

More Details
Selection of a healthcare facility to host a workshop

We have established partnerships with the largest and most renowned Italian hospitals. This said, we constantly expand our network in order to have direct contacts with new excellent healthcare facilities. Once a medical specialization is defined, we reach out to the most fitting healthcare centers to check their availability to host an internship and to further develop a plan of training with our organizational support. This enables us to meet the needs of a group, offering the highest added value possible.

More Details
More than just workshops

Healthcare institutions can benefit from our existing partnerships and take advantage of the already defined international agreements on education, training and research. Yet, upon request, these agreements may be rendered "tailor made" in order to better respond to the needs of the parties concerned.

More Details
Customized workshops

Each workshop is customized based on the needs of the parties. We currently organize seminars lasting up to one week for a group of up to 20 participants. Each workshop is organized in such a way as to provide exchange of scientific experience with renowned representatives of Italian medicine. Normally our seminars are divided into two parts. The first one is dedicated to live case studies, whereas the second is all about discussion of what has been learned.

More Details

Objectives and strategies

Impactful training

Each workshop is custom-made and unique as it takes into consideration the skills of every single participant. For this we cannot but thank the host institutions which at this stage of the process become of essential support and have always given us the greatest cooperation. We believe this is the key to create such unique, targeted and effective programs. Each our training is invariably finished with genuine applause of the participants, and this is the best reward for our work.

More Details
Full-fledged organization

Ci dedichiamo con attenzione maniacale all'impostazione della parte scientifica, ma non dimentichiamo che un evento organizzato in ambito internazionale ha bisogno di molto altro per la sua riuscita. Anche in questo nulla può essere lasciato al caso, ed è per questo che, con partner selezionati, con i quali collaboriamo in modo costante e continuativo, la nostra offerta formativa risulta alla fine essere completa a 360 gradi, a partire dal servizio di interpretariato in simultanea, in ogni fase del seminario, e seguire con organizzazione dei voli, visti d'ingresso, trasferimenti, hotel, breakfast, lunch e dinner Chi frequenta lo stage e chi lo svolge, non devono avere e non avranno mai alcun tipo di pensiero oltre allo stage stesso.

More Details
Innovative service

We aim for excellence, we search for excellence, we offer excellence. Our work is to organize international scientific exchange as we follow every stage of this process, from preparation to implementation. The programs are set up, studied and defined, involving both the applicant and the host, under our guidance and support. Our engagement is continuous, as we are physically present at each training. Our activity must be carefully defined because events we organize require a high level of professionalism. International exchange in any field brings important changes and opens opportunities for growth, and in the medical field it offers to the scientific community the opportunity to fuse experiences and create synergy. Evaluation, study and improvement of processes, treatments and organization – this is how we identify ourselves, and in all this we aim for excellence.

More Details
Organization and supervising of every single day of the training

Each day of an internship is divided into practical and theoretical part. Not only do we pay attention to scientific topics, but also to the organizational ones – from the most specific aspects, related to the specialty of participants, to the general principles of work of the hosting institution. It goes without saying that the practical part can only have an observational character, whether with in-person presence in the operating theatre, or from comfortable classrooms in the format of live surgery remote broadcast with the possibility to interact, ask questions and share experience with the foreign team. Both lectures and discussions of clinical cases are accompanied by presentations in Italian or English, which are then shared among the participants via USB-drive. We provide simultaneous interpretation of lectures into the native language of participants.
